I can register your Advent of Code name, and then automatically invite you into the daily chat rooms once you solve each daily problem\.

Supported commands:

/reg \<aocname\>
    Register your Advent of Code name\. Format your name exactly as it is visible in our
    [leaderboard](https://adventofcode\.com/2021/leaderboard/private/view/380635) \(without the `(AoC\+\+)` suffix\)\.

/unreg
    Unregister from the bot\.

/status
    Display your registration status\.

/update \[today\|all\|\<year\>\|\<year\> \<day\>\]
    Process leaderboards and invites\. Updates are performed automatically every 15 minutes\.
    This command is only needed if you want to trigger an update immediately\.

    /update
    /update today
        Process today's leaderboard and invites \(default in December\)

    /update
    /update year
        Process most recent year's leaderboards and invites \(default outside of December\)

    /update \<year\>
        Process leaderboards and invites for selected year

    /update \<day\>
        Process leaderboards and invites for selected day in current year

    /update \<day\> \<year\>
    /update \<year\> \<day\>
        Process leaderboards and invites for selected day


/board \[today\|\<day\>\|\<year\> \<day\|\<day\> \<year\>
    Format and display a board for selected day\.

    /board
    /board today
        Display today's board

    /board \<day\>
        Display board for selected day in current year

    /board \<day\> \<year\>
    /board \<year\> \<day\>
         Display board for selected day and year

/logs on\|off
    Enable/disable sending activity logs into your chat with the bot\.

/logs
    Find out whether sending activity logs is currently enabled or disabled for your chat with the bot\.

/help
    Show this message\.
